About

  • October 2019

    Impact 2050 Hackathon

    Impact 2050™ is an integrated and innovative multi-year entrepreneurial programme that aims to catalyze the impact entrepreneurship ecosystem in Asia Pacific. Innovators across the Philippines created digital solutions through a 24hrs reverse hackathon targeted towards the Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs). Our Team was formed and created a agritech solution called Transfarm, a marketplace platform made in a bid to bridge the gap between local producers and commercial consumers

  • November 2019

    Transfarm is Born

    Our group won First Place in the Impact 2050™ Hackathon Regional Event (Manila). We then competed in the National Event with the top 15 finalist from all over the country. We also won first place in the nationals. We bagged 170,000 php (70,000 of which was from winning the Manila leg of the hackathon) in cash prize and a year-long incubation program (valued at 1.5M pesos) translating to global membership access to the Impact Hub network, intensive training, access to vast pool of expert mentors, network, and coworking.
